About Errand Boys


Bringing Groceries to your doorstep since 2018

Errand Boys is a small scale grocery delivery service based in Knoxville, TN. Our deliveries are based on orders placed online using this website. We service a small area that includes about five grocery stores around the University of Tennessee Campus. Once deliveries are placed online, one of our three employees will drive to the grocery store chosen by the customer, buy the items listed on the customer’s order then deliver the groceries to the customer’s desired address. Payment is completed upon delivery. This service aims to assist those who do not have the time during their day to go to the grocery store and also people who are elderly or disabled and unable to shop for themselves. Errand Boys is committed to speedy delivery, excellence in customer satisfaction, efficiency, and safety.

Payment and Cost Information

Payment is not required until your groceries are delivered completely to your satisfaction. The cost of this service is structured as follows: $15 for 30 Items or Less and an additional $2.50 for every 15 Items after that. Cash, Credit and Debit Cards, Mobile Payment Applications and Checks will be accepted as payment upon delivery. Orders will be delivered one to two hours after they are placed. If you need to cancel an order, please cancel it within 30 minutes of completing it online. If the order is not cancelled within 30 minutes of placement, then your order will be delivered on time with payment expected.
